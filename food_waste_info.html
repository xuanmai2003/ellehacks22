Current situation in Canada

Canada is one of the worst countries globally in terms of food waste, according to a report on the state of food loss and waste in Canada, U.S. and Mexico, with 396 kilograms of food per capita wasted in Canada every year. In Canada, almost 2.2 million tonnes of edible food are wasted annually. The cost to Canadians? An excess of 17 billion dollars. Wanted food not only wastes the time and resources used to grow, produce and distribute the food, it also contributes significantly to climate change. 841,191 Canadians rely on food banks every month The number of people needing food assistance in Canada has not dropped below 700,000 a month in about 15 years
