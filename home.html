Home Food shortage and food wastage What you can do to help?

During the Covid-19 pandemic, access to basic needs has been a challenge for many people. Food wastage is still happening while many struggle to have a decent meal. We came up with the idea of a mobile form of a food bank:

FOOD VENDING MACHINE

How does it work?

People, who were given an ID number by Health Services that verifies them as people in need for food, can type in there number at these vending machines and they can receive a bag of hot meal or grocery pack directly.
